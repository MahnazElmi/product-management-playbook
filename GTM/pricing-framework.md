# Product Pricing Framework

Pricing should reflect customer value, not development cost.

---

# Common Pricing Models

## Freemium

Best for:

- SaaS
- AI Products

Pros

- Fast adoption

Cons

- Low conversion

---

## Subscription

Monthly

Yearly

Usage-based

Seat-based

---

## Usage-Based

Examples

- API Calls
- Tokens
- Minutes
- Storage

Suitable for AI products.

---

## Value-Based Pricing

Price according to the value delivered.

Example

Save customer:

20 hours/month

Value:

$2,000/month

Price:

$200/month

---

# Pricing Checklist

Understand customer willingness to pay

Research competitors

Estimate unit economics

Validate with customers

Run pricing experiments

Monitor conversion

Review regularly
