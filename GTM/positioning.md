# Product Positioning Framework

Positioning defines why customers should choose your product over alternatives.

---

## Positioning Statement

For:

(Target Audience)

Who:

(Customer Problem)

Our Product:

(Category)

Provides:

(Core Value)

Unlike:

(Main Competitor)

We:

(Differentiator)

---

## Example

For Product Managers

who struggle to organize product knowledge,

PM Playbook

is an open-source resource hub

that provides practical frameworks and reusable templates.

Unlike scattered blog posts,

PM Playbook offers structured, actionable documentation.
