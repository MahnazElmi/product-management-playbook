# LLM SEO (AI Search Optimization)

A practical guide to optimizing websites for Large Language Models (LLMs) such as ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, and other AI-powered search experiences.

---

# What is LLM SEO?

LLM SEO is the practice of making your content easier for AI models to discover, understand, trust, and cite when answering user questions.

Unlike traditional SEO, ranking #1 on Google is no longer the only goal.

The new objective is to become a trusted source that AI assistants reference in their responses.

---

# How AI Search Works

Most AI assistants evaluate content based on signals such as:

- Authority
- Expertise
- Freshness
- Structured information
- Citations from trusted websites
- Semantic relevance
- Clear entity relationships

Instead of matching keywords, AI models attempt to understand concepts.

---

# Core Principles

## 1. Write for Questions

Instead of targeting keywords only, answer real user questions.

Example:

❌ Voice AI Platform

✅ How do Voice AI platforms reduce customer support costs?

---

## 2. Build Entity Authority

Focus on becoming authoritative around a topic.

Example entities:

- Voice AI
- Conversational AI
- AI Agents
- Product Management
- Technical SEO

Create multiple interconnected articles around each entity.

---

## 3. Use Clear Structure

Prefer:

- Headings
- Bullet lists
- Tables
- Definitions
- Examples
- Step-by-step explanations

Well-structured content is easier for LLMs to understand.

---

## 4. Add Original Insights

AI models value content that contains:

- Research
- Data
- Benchmarks
- Case studies
- First-hand experience

Avoid publishing generic AI-generated articles.

---

## 5. Demonstrate Expertise

Include:

- Author information
- Industry experience
- Real examples
- Product screenshots
- Original diagrams

---

## 6. Keep Content Updated

AI systems prefer recent and maintained content.

Review important pages regularly.

---

## 7. Improve Internal Linking

Connect related pages naturally.

Example:

Product Metrics
↓

North Star Metrics
↓

OKRs
↓

Product Strategy

---

## 8. Create Helpful Resources

Examples:

- Templates
- Checklists
- Frameworks
- Calculators
- Open-source repositories

Useful resources earn citations.

- Fast website
- Mobile-friendly
- Clean HTML
- Structured headings
- Descriptive titles
- Schema.org markup
- Canonical URLs
- XML Sitemap
- HTTPS

---

# Content Checklist

Before publishing, ask:

- Does this answer a real question?
- Is it better than existing content?
- Does it include original insights?
- Is it easy to scan?
- Is it updated?
- Would I cite this page if I were an AI assistant?
